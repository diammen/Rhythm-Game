# Rhythm-Game
How to play:\
press A key to hit first lane\
press S key to hit second lane\
press K key to hit third lane\
press L key to hit fourth lane\
wait until the white square lands on the pink square before hitting

rules:\
three timings:
1. Perfect
2. Great
3. Miss

bug(s): none
