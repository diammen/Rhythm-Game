How to play:  
press A key to hit first lane  
press S key to hit second lane  
press K key to hit third lane  
press L key to hit fourth lane  
wait until the white square lands on the pink square before hitting  
You have the option to change the keys if you wish in the main menu  
Press enter to navigate through the menu

rules:
three timings:  
Perfect  
Great  
Miss

Song used:  
THE DAY - Porno Graffitti  
Great Days - Karen Aoki & Daisuke Hasegawa
